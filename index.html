DSM-5 Classification and Treatment Options

The DSM-5 (Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, 5th Edition) is a widely used manual for diagnosing mental health disorders. It provides a classification system and criteria for various disorders. The treatment options for each disorder may vary and can include therapy, medication, lifestyle changes, and support services. It's important to consult with a mental health professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment recommendations.
